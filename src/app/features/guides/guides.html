map Battle Phases

Phase 1 covers all outer and central locations available from the start. Focus on securing your assigned position before Phase 2 begins.

Phase 2 unlocks additional locations: Mercenary Camp, Munitions Warehouse, Imperial Foundry, and all Arsenal Supply depots.

 groups Legions

Players are assigned to Legion 1 or Legion 2 by the admin. Each legion operates independently with its own assignments and battle plan.

Check your Personal Plan to see which legion you're in and what your tasks are.

 person_search Finding Your Plan

Go to Personal Plan and enter your in-game ID to see your specific assignments for each phase.

Your in-game ID is the numeric ID shown in your game profile (e.g. 12345678).

 public Global Map

The Global Plan shows the full battle map with all player assignments for a selected legion and phase.

Use it to coordinate positions and avoid overlap with teammates.
